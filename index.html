Herman's Puzzle Hunt Resources

Note: This page is still under construction and subject to change in the near future. More content will be added.

Hi! I'm Herman Chau who competed in many puzzle hunts with the team Black Fedora Group. I've written some resources to help new puzzle hunters quickly get up to speed on the types of puzzles they might encounter in a puzzle hunt and I've also authored a number of puzzle hunts.

On this site you'll find the documents I've written for both new puzzle hunters and to record mistakes that we (Black Fedora Group) have made in past hunts. You'll also find puzzles I've written and their corresponding solutions here.

PDFs

  • Puzzlehunt Guide: A document that aims to cover useful tools for puzzlehunts, interpersonal team strategies, and a field guide to different puzzle types.
  • Have you tried carrot: A document with mistakes that Black Fedora Group has committed in past puzzle hunts. Each mistake has the corresponding puzzle next to it.

Miscellaneous Links

  • Huntress Reverse Image Search: A Chrome extension I wrote that aids in identifying images in puzzlehunts. The primary feature that distinguishes this from other methods of reverse image searching is the ability to easily crop out a section of an image to search. This lets you reverse image search pdfs, videos, or composited images.
